    • The Internet of things

    In simple terms, the Internet of things basically refers to the integration of the Internet and communication technology into daily life through everyday household items. As technology improves and advances, people are naturally going to be more dependent on it for their daily lives, as such, a recent trend in the industry has been to to take any common household item and attempt to integrate it into the Internet.

    \

    Some recent additions to the Internet of thinsg include:

    • Thermostats
    • Doorbells
    • Weighing Scales

    However, while this may all seem well and good, it may very well be the case that not all households items are created equal. And with the industry's strategy of "spray and pray" some items have seen unprecedented success, while others have fallen completely flat on their faces. All in all, the internet of things has the potential to completely revolutionise life as we know it, just... have a little common sense.
